As technology develops, the need for technology to be implemented into the classroom also needs to coincide, as the future generations will need to have more technical mind sets. The use of technology has evolved in classrooms, rapidly and it shows no signs of slowing down. Students are now so used to technology being in their lives in the forms of mobile phones and tablets and games consoles, and in many schools, it is expected that there is some form of technology being used to develop the education level is students (Education City, 2019).
